OSINT Query Guide (How to Search)

  • Exact Match: Use quotes to force exact wording
    "John Michael Doe"
  • OR Logic: Finds aliases or misspellings
    "Jon Doe" OR "John Doe"
  • site: Search one website only
    John Doe site:facebook.com
  • -Exclude: Remove noise terms
    John Doe -obituary -memorial
  • intitle: Term appears in page title
    intitle:"John Doe"
  • inurl: Appears in URL
    inurl:johndoe
  • filetype: Find documents
    John Doe filetype:pdf
  • Date Ranges: Narrow by years
    John Doe 2020..2024
  • Usernames: Pivot across platforms
    "johndoe87"
